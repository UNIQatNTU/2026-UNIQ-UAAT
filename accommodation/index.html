Accommodation

The conference has blocked the rooms for attendees at the Howard Civil Service International House, which is located on the campus of National Taiwan University. The Howard Civil Service International House is a convenient and comfortable accommodation option for conference attendees, offering easy access to the conference venue and a range of amenities. The hotel features well-appointed rooms with modern furnishings, free Wi-Fi, and 24-hour front desk service. Attendees can enjoy the convenience of being on campus, with easy access to the conference sessions and events, as well as the opportunity to experience the vibrant campus atmosphere. The hotel also offers a variety of dining options, including a restaurant and a café, making it easy for attendees to grab a meal or a quick snack between sessions. We encourage attendees to take advantage of the special room rates offered for conference attendees and to book their accommodations early, as availability may be limited.
