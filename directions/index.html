Directions

The workshop will be held at R417 in the MKT Innovation Building on the campus of National Taiwan University in Taipei, Taiwan.

The MKT Innovation Building is located in the northeastern part of the NTU campus, near the intersection of Roosevelt Road and Keelung Road. The building is easily accessible by public transportation, and there are several nearby bus stops and a metro station within walking distance. For attendees arriving by car, there are parking facilities available on campus, although we recommend using public transportation due to limited parking spaces and traffic congestion in the area. We encourage attendees to plan their routes in advance and to allow extra time for travel, especially during peak hours. For those coming from outside of Taipei, the city is well-connected by high-speed rail, regular train services, and an international airport, making it convenient for attendees to reach the conference venue from various locations.
